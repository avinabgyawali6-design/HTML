Flowers

Flowers are also called the bloom or blossom of a plant. Flowers have petals. Inside the part of the flower that has petals are the parts which produce pollen and seeds. A poster of flowers produced by twelve species of flowering plants from different families. In all plants, a flower is usually its most colourful part.
