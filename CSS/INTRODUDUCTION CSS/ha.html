CODINGAL

Codingal is an online education platform specializing in coding and AI classes for K-12 students (ages 5–18). It focuses on project-based learning to help kids build games, apps, and websites while developing logical thinking and problem-solving skills.

    Founded: September 2020 by Vivek Prakash and Satyam Baranwal.

    Backing: Supported by Y Combinator and prominent angel investors.

    Mission: To inspire every child to fall in love with coding and AI through creative exploration
