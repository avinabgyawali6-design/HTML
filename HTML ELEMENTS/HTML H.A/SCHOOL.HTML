The name of my school is New Horizon English Boarding Secodary School
 New Horizon English Boarding Secondary School, located in Butwal, Nepal, is a well-established institution founded in 1989 (2046 B.S.) that focuses on providing quality education at an affordable cost. The school is widely recognized for its academic excellence, having produced multiple national-level toppers (SLC Board 1st) and receiving the Western Region Education Shield six times. [1, 2, 3]
